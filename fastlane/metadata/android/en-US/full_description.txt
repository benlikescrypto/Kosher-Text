Kosher Text is your trusted messaging companion, designed to enhance your messaging experience in various ways.

📱 STAY CONNECTED WITH EASE:
With Kosher Text, you can send SMS and MMS, stay in touch with people you care about, and use group messaging with photos, emojis, and quick greetings.

🚫 BLOCK UNWANTED MESSAGES:
Control who can reach you with strong blocking, including unknown senders, and export or import block lists. You can also filter messages that contain specific words or phrases.

🔒 EFFORTLESS SMS BACKUP:
Export and import messages so you can switch devices without losing important conversations.

🚀 LIGHTNING-FAST AND LIGHTWEIGHT:
A small app that stays quick to install and use.

🔐 ENHANCED PRIVACY:
Choose what appears on your lock screen—sender, content, or nothing.

🔍 EFFICIENT MESSAGE SEARCH:
Find messages quickly with built-in search.

🌈 MODERN DESIGN:
A clear interface with a dark theme option.

🌐 OPEN-SOURCE:
No ads or tracking for core use; the code is here for you to read and build yourself.

Source code, issues, and contributions: this repository on GitHub.
